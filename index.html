Football Club Potential

EFL Revenue Growth & Expansion Tracker

🎯 Map Purpose: This model evaluates football clubs as long-term commercial assets and commercial real estate operations. It measures an asset's capacity to scale non-football and match-day revenue streams under corporate investment. It is completely decoupled from temporary on-pitch performance or relegation form guides.

Investment & Growth Framework

  • ⭐ Brand Pull: The asset's intrinsic global commercial draw. More stars indicate pre-existing customer demand waiting to be leveraged once physical bottlenecks clear.
  • 🏟️ Capacity vs Attendance: Highlights infrastructure optimisation room. Low utilisation scores reveal opportunities to grow immediate gate lines by improving sports marketing without heavy capital build-out costs.
  • 🌱 Talent Pipeline: The club's Elite Player Performance Plan (EPPP) Academy status. High-category facilities enable capital-exempt youth development and generate 100% pure accounting profit through player trading.
  • 🚀 Growth Headroom: Physical space and zoning allowances to structurally expand stadium capacity or stadium operations under sustainability rules.
  • 🛡️ Local Competition: Regional market cluster density, tracking risk of consumer base dilution.

* Live active configurations mapped as of 2026. Temporary stands undergoing full construction updates are dynamically throttled back to live physical limits.
